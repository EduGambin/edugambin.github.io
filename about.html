Experience

Currently I am working as a C++ Software Engineer at Amadeus (contracted by Alten). My job is focused on optimizing revenue for airlines by predicting the best value for a flight ticket. This prediction is performed by a mathematical model, which uses contextual data to adjust the final price of the ticket.

In the past I have done internships on three separate occasions as a DevOps Engineer at Orenes, where I was put in charge of developing automated testing for performance impacts and incorporating them in the already existing CI pipeline. The system is still in use today by the company.

In my free time, when I was doing both of my bachelor's degrees, I was a freelance private tutor for other university students.
